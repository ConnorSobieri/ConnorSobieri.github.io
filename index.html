What Are Vampire Monkeys?

Vampire Monkeys, scientifically called, " nutrientibus sanguis", are a very rare species of monkey. Unlike other monkey species, the Vampire Monkey is a predator and sucks on the blood of its prey for nutrients, hence the name, "Vampire Monkey". Differentiating traits include, razor sharp teeth and bright red eyes. However, the main differentiator would be the liquid blood dripping off of its mouth or fur. Other than these small things, they have very similar traits to other types of monkeys, however they have adaptations that make every part of their body vicious. They have razor sharp claws, even sharper pieces of bone sticking out of their tails, fur that is poisonous to the touch when they are hunting or feel they are in danger, and large muscles in their legs that allow them to run at over 30mph and have a kick stronger than a Kangaroo. These monkeys are also known to be very intelligent as they use tools such as magic bow and arrows, swords laced with poison, tiny shurikens, and rocket launchers. They have also been recently discovered to be in communication with an equally rare and vicious species, known as the Allison Grizzly Bear. The Vampire Monkeys are carnivores and have a diet consisting of the blood of squirrels, African or European Swallows, and annoying siblings and neighbors. They have also adapted to travel in packs and are able to camoflauge in any environment, as they gang up on their prey and split up the food rewards. They are also recently been found to find pleasure in the hunt of their prey as they have been hunting for one specific human, Roman Sobieri, for decades.
