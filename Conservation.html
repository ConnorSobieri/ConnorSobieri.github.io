Conservation

While the Vampire Monkey is clearly a threat to humans, as they seem themselves as our predator, it is important that we conserve them, as with the draining of the Binney Park lake and and a new war with the piranhas from the Amazon River, they are considered to be an endangered species. This is bad for the local Greenwich ecosystem as they are the apex predator. Therefore the population of squirrels, African or European Swallows, and annoying neighbors increases exponentially. Therefore the increase of their population causes major decreases in other populations and causes more problems for humans.
